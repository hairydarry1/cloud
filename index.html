II.About / Manifesto•Cloud / Studio 01002 / 008
About the studio · Nº 02

Clothing is freedom, not constraint — we make the lightest silhouette possible.

Cloud was born from a simple idea: what if clothing felt as light as a cloud? We work with natural fibers, oversized cuts, and organic dyes to create pieces that let you move through your day without thinking about what you're wearing.

Capabilities · Nº 03

Fabrics, forms, and silhouettes that let you breathe.

Every Cloud piece is designed around the body in motion — generous proportions, considered fabrics, and a palette drawn from the sky.

V.Method / Design Loop•04 stages, iterative005 / 008
Method · Nº 05

From dreams to wearable form.

+

Every stage is iterative, tactile, and research-driven — files and fabrics, not briefs and guesswork.

01

Dream →

We collect signals — art, architecture, street photography, the way light falls through a window.

02

Design →

Sketches become digital patterns. Every seam is questioned. Every drape is photographed.

03

Make →

Sample rooms in Lisbon and Tokyo. We fit on real bodies across sizes, shapes, and heights.

04

Release

Limited drops, no seasons. Each piece ships with a care story, a QR code, and a return bag.

Fabrics inform everything. Movement makes it real.
